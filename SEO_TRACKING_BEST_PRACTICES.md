## ğŸ” Keywords Tracking

### **Industry Standards (2026)**

According to industry research:
- **Small sites**: Average 20-30 keywords ([SmartClick Agency](https://smartclick.agency/blog/tracking-keywords-seo/))

### **GeoVera Recommendation: 50-100 Keywords per Brand**

#### **Tier-Based Breakdown:**

**Basic Tier (50 keywords):**
- 10 **Gold keywords** (primary, high-value): Brand terms, money keywords

**Premium Tier (75 keywords):**
- 15 **Gold keywords**
- 30 **Silver keywords**
- 30 **Bronze keywords**

**Partner Tier (100 keywords):**
- 20 **Gold keywords**
- 40 **Silver keywords**
- 40 **Bronze keywords**

## ğŸ”— Backlinks Tracking

### **Industry Standards (2026)**

Key findings from research:
- **Focus on referring domains** over total backlinks ([Respona](https://respona.com/blog/how-to-monitor-backlinks/))
- **Monitor monthly** minimum ([Editorial.link](https://editorial.link/backlink-management/))
- **Track dofollow ratio**: 70% dofollow, 30% nofollow ([Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/hub/seo/metrics))

### **GeoVera Recommendation: Track 100-200 Referring Domains**

**Why Referring Domains > Total Backlinks?**
- 1 high-quality domain linking 100 times = same value as 1 link
- Referring domains = more accurate SEO impact
- Easier to manage and monitor quality

#### **Tier-Based Breakdown:**

**Basic Tier (100 referring domains):**
- Focus on top 100 highest-authority domains linking to you
- Track new/lost domains weekly
- Alert on toxic links monthly

**Premium Tier (150 referring domains):**
- Top 150 referring domains
- Competitor backlink gap analysis
- Daily new/lost alerts

**Partner Tier (200 referring domains):**
- Top 200 referring domains
- Full backlink profile analysis
- Real-time alerts (new, lost, toxic)
- Competitor backlink monitoring

### **Backlink Priority Classification:**

```
ğŸ”¥ TIER 1 (High Authority):
- DR/DA 70+ domains
- Government (.gov), Education (.edu)
- Major news sites, industry publications
- Brand mentions with links

â­ TIER 2 (Medium Authority):
- DR/DA 40-69 domains
- Industry blogs, niche publications
- Directory listings (high-quality)
- Guest post opportunities

âœ… TIER 3 (Growing Authority):
- DR/DA 20-39 domains
- New blogs in your niche
- Social profiles (high engagement)
- Emerging content platforms
```

### **Key Metrics to Track per Backlink:**

1. **Domain Rating/Authority** (Ahrefs DR, Moz DA)
2. **Link Type** (dofollow/nofollow)
3. **Anchor Text** (branded, exact match, generic)
4. **Link Context** (editorial, footer, sidebar)
5. **Traffic Estimate** (referring domain traffic)
6. **Spam Score** (toxicity rating)
7. **Link Status** (active, lost, broken)
8. **First Seen Date** (when link appeared)

### **Monitoring Frequency:**

- **Real-time**: Toxic link alerts
- **Daily**: New/lost backlinks (Premium/Partner tiers)
- **Weekly**: Backlink health report (Basic tier)
- **Monthly**: Full backlink audit
- **Quarterly**: Competitor backlink gap analysis

### **Dofollow/Nofollow Ratio Target:**

- **Target**: 70% dofollow, 30% nofollow
- **Alert if**: <60% dofollow (unnatural link profile)
- **Alert if**: >90% dofollow (potential spam)

## ğŸ‘¥ Competitor Tracking

### **Industry Standards (2026)**

Research findings:
- **Track 3-5 main competitors** ([Causal Funnel](https://www.causalfunnel.com/blog/seo-benchmarking-2026-11-proven-metrics-to-track-beat-competitors-boost-rankings-2/))
- **Mix competitor sizes** (established + emerging) ([Siteimprove](https://www.siteimprove.com/glossary/seo-competitor-analysis/))
- **Monthly monitoring** + **quarterly deep-dive** ([Competitive Intelligence Alliance](https://www.competitiveintelligencealliance.io/seo-competitive-analysis/))

### **GeoVera Recommendation: Track 3-5 Direct Competitors**

#### **Competitor Selection Strategy:**

**3 Competitors (Basic Tier):**
1. **Market Leader** (biggest competitor in your category)
2. **Direct Rival** (similar size/positioning)
3. **Emerging Threat** (fastest-growing competitor)

**4 Competitors (Premium Tier):**
1. **Market Leader**
2. **Direct Rival #1**
3. **Direct Rival #2**
4. **Emerging Threat**

**5 Competitors (Partner Tier):**
1. **Market Leader**
2. **Direct Rival #1**
3. **Direct Rival #2**
4. **Emerging Threat #1**
5. **Niche Disruptor** (innovative competitor)

### **How to Identify Competitors:**

1. **Keyword Overlap**: Domains appearing in top 10 for your gold keywords
2. **Search Console**: Google's "Similar sites" in GSC
3. **Backlink Overlap**: Domains sharing similar backlink sources
4. **Category Leaders**: Top brands in your industry
5. **User Perception**: Ask customers "who else did you consider?"

### **Competitor Metrics to Track:**

```
ğŸ“Š RANKING METRICS:
- Keyword overlap (how many shared keywords?)
- Average position (are they ranking higher?)
- Keyword gap (keywords they rank for, you don't)
- Visibility score (overall search visibility)

ğŸ”— BACKLINK METRICS:
- Referring domains count
- Domain authority/rating
- Backlink gap (links they have, you don't)
- Link velocity (new links per month)

ğŸ“ˆ CONTENT METRICS:
- Content volume (how many pages?)
- Content frequency (publishing rate)
- Content types (blog, video, infographics)
- Top-performing content (most shared/linked)

âš¡ PERFORMANCE METRICS:
- Estimated organic traffic
- Traffic trends (growing/declining?)
- Top landing pages
- SERP features won (snippets, PAA, videos)
```

### **Monitoring Frequency:**

- **Weekly**: Keyword position changes (top 20 shared keywords)
- **Monthly**: Full competitor dashboard review
- **Quarterly**: Deep-dive competitor analysis
- **Annually**: Strategic competitor landscape review

### **Competitive Alerts:**

- âš ï¸ **Alert if**: Competitor gains 5+ positions on gold keywords
- âš ï¸ **Alert if**: Competitor gains 20+ new backlinks in 1 week
- âš ï¸ **Alert if**: Competitor publishes content on your target keyword
- âš ï¸ **Alert if**: Competitor's traffic grows >20% month-over-month
