## üéØ WHY 18-25% MARGIN WORKS

### **1. Intelligent Cost Optimization**

#### **A. Delta Caching (70% Hit Rate)**
- **Without cache**: 100 checks √ó $0.08 = **$8.00**
- **With cache**: 30 checks √ó $0.08 = **$2.40**
- **Savings**: **70% ($5.60)**

#### **B. Smart AI Selection**
- Use **Gemini Flash Lite** ($0.01/1M tokens) for indexing instead of GPT-4o ($0.15/1M)
- **Savings**: **93%**

#### **C. Batch Processing**
- Process 100 items at once vs 100 separate API calls
- **Savings**: **40% (reduced overhead)**
