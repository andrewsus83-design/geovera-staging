# Daily Insights Task Types Documentation

## Overview
The Daily Insights system generates up to **8/10/12 tasks per day** (based on tier) across **15+ task types**. Each task is data-driven, actionable, and includes clear "why" explanations with measurable outcomes.

---

## Task Categories

### 1. CRISIS RESPONSE
**Priority Level:** Urgent/High

#### 1.1 Viral Negative Mentions
- **Trigger:** 50+ negative mentions with 100K+ reach in 24h
- **Why:** Rapid negative sentiment spreading across social platforms
- **Expected Outcome:** Crisis communication plan executed, negative mentions reduced by 50%
- **Deadline:** 2 hours (critical severity)

#### 1.2 Sentiment Crash
- **Trigger:** -30% sentiment change in 48 hours
- **Why:** Rapid negative sentiment shift threatens brand perception
- **Expected Outcome:** Root cause identified, crisis communication plan prepared
- **Deadline:** 4 hours

#### 1.3 Ranking Crash (Keyword)
- **Trigger:** -5+ positions for priority keywords in 48h
- **Why:** Severe ranking drop impacts organic traffic
- **Expected Outcome:** Emergency SEO optimization implemented
- **Deadline:** 24 hours

#### 1.4 Competitor Surge
- **Trigger:** Competitor gains +40% marketshare in 7 days
- **Why:** Rapid competitor growth threatens competitive position
- **Expected Outcome:** Deep-dive competitor analysis, counter-strategy launched
- **Deadline:** 48 hours

#### 1.5 GEO Score Crash
- **Trigger:** -15 points GEO score in 24 hours
- **Why:** Local SEO visibility loss impacts discovery
- **Expected Outcome:** GMB profile optimized, reviews responded
- **Deadline:** 2 hours (critical)

## Priority Scoring Algorithm

```typescript
Priority Score (0-100) =
  Base Priority (40%) +
  Impact Score (30%) +
  Time Sensitivity (15%) +
  Data Confidence (5%) +
  Category Weight (5%) +
  Recency Bonus (5%)
```

### Base Priority Scores
- **Urgent:** 90
- **High:** 70
- **Medium:** 50
- **Low:** 30

### Category Weights (0-10)
- **Crisis Response:** 10
- **Competitor Intelligence:** 8
- **Performance Decline:** 8
- **SEO Alert:** 7
- **Local SEO Alert:** 7
- **Trend Opportunity:** 6
- **AI Recommendation:** 6
- **Market Opportunity:** 6
- **Keyword Opportunity:** 5
- **Content Optimization:** 4
- **Partnership Opportunity:** 4
- **Content Publishing:** 3
- **Content Promotion:** 2
- **Content Creation:** 2

### Time Sensitivity (0-20)
- **<24 hours:** 20 (Extremely urgent)
- **24-48 hours:** 15 (Very urgent)
- **48-72 hours:** 10 (Urgent)
- **1 week:** 5 (Moderate)
- **>1 week:** 0 (Low)

## Task Diversity Rules

1. **Crisis Tasks:** Always included (up to 3 max)
2. **Category Diversity:** Max 4 tasks per category
3. **Source Diversity:** Max 5 tasks per data source (radar, search, hub, chat)
4. **Priority Balance:** Mix of urgent, high, medium, low priorities
5. **Actionability:** Every task has clear next steps

---

## Success Metrics

Each task includes:
- **Clear "Why":** Data-driven reasoning (200-300 chars)
- **Expected Outcome:** Measurable result
- **Success Metrics:** Specific targets with timeframes
- **Deadline:** Calculated based on priority
- **Impact Score:** Expected business impact (0-100)
- **Confidence Score:** Data reliability (0.0-1.0)
