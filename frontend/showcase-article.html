Why It Works: The Power of Verification-First

üéØ Key Insight: Garbage In = Garbage Out

Traditional AI workflows start with indexing, which allows models to hallucinate "facts" that propagate through the entire pipeline. By verifying first with Perplexity's real-time search, we establish a foundation of truth that prevents downstream hallucination.

The secret is in how we prompt Perplexity Step 0:

  1. Focus on 6 critical fields only ‚Äî Parent company, website, Instagram, category, product type, launch year. Less is more.
  2. Use country-specific search terms ‚Äî "Kata Oma Indonesia produsen" finds the right brand, not US companies with similar names.
  3. Demand source verification ‚Äî Perplexity must cite Indonesian business news, company registrations, press releases.
  4. Accept "Unable to verify" ‚Äî Better to admit uncertainty than to hallucinate confidently.
