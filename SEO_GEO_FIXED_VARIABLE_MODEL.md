## üîÑ SEO & GEO: TIERED MONITORING MODEL (7/14/28 Day Cycles)

- **SSO (Social)**: Content changes daily ‚Üí 3D/7D/14D cycles ‚úÖ
- **SEO (Search)**: Rankings shift weekly/monthly ‚Üí 7D/14D/28D cycles ‚úÖ
- **GEO (AI Engines)**: Citation patterns stable ‚Üí 7D/14D/28D cycles ‚úÖ
